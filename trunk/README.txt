befoo - IMAP4 mail checker <http://sourceforge.jp/projects/befoo/>

Introduction:
-------------
Befoo is a simple IMAP4/POP3 client for Windows 2000 or later. It fetches
mail from plural mailbox, and shows summary with subject, sender and date.
You can read non-ascii subjects if the iconv.dll was installed.


Installation:
-------------
You will copy the file "befoo.exe" and "extend.dll" to your application
folder. And you may make a shortcut in the startup folder.
